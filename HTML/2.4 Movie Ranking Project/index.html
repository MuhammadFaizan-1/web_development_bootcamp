The Best Movies According To Faizan

My top 3 movies of all-time

Interstellar

▪️Amazing space visuals and science concepts
▪️Powerful father–daughter emotional bond.
▪️Music and ending leave a huge impact.

The Shawshank Redemption

▪️Story of friendship and hope in prison.
▪️ Inspiring escape and freedom journey.
▪️ Teaches patience and never giving up.

Titanic

▪️ Epic love story between Jack and Rose.
▪️ Stunning visuals of the ship and tragedy.
▪️ Mix of romance, sacrifice, and disaster.

Shutter Island

▪️Intense mystery and psychological tension.
▪️ Keeps you guessing till the end.
▪️ Shocking twist changes the whole story.

Avengers: Infinity War

▪️Huge team of superheroes together.
▪️Epic fight against Thanos. Emotional ending
▪️ with big sacrifices.
